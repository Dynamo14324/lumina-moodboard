## â“ COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: Why did I get errors on /terms and /sitemap?
A: Those aren't valid sitemap paths. /terms is a page. /sitemap is missing .xml
   extension. Only /sitemap.xml is the correct sitemap.

Q: Do I need to add /privacy or /terms to sitemaps?
A: No! These pages are not in your sitemap.ts file. They're static pages that
   Google will find through normal crawling. You can add them if you want, but
   it's not required.

Q: Should I submit robots.txt as a sitemap?
A: No! robots.txt is NOT a sitemap. It just POINTS to your sitemap. Google
   will find the sitemap automatically from robots.txt.

Q: How do I add /privacy and /terms to the sitemap?
A: You need to edit src/app/sitemap.ts and add them to the return array. But
   this is optional - Google will find them via normal crawling.

Q: My sitemap shows "Couldn't fetch" - what's wrong?
A: Either:
   1. Wrong path (use sitemap.xml, not sitemap)
   2. Site is down (unlikely)
   3. DNS issue (temporary)
   Wait 24 hours and Google will retry automatically.
